Spodumene

Color: Green, Purple, Yellow, Blue, White, Pink, Grey, Orange, Brown
Luster: Vitreous
Crystal system: Monoclinic crystal system
Chemical formula: LiAlSi₂O₆
Hardness (Mohs hardness scale): 6.5 – 7
Transparency: Transparent
Refractive index: 1.66 – 1.68

Spodumene is a pyroxene mineral consisting of lithium aluminum inosilicate, LiAl(SiO₃)₂, and is a source of lithium. It occurs as colorless to yellowish, purplish, or lilac kunzite, yellowish-green or emerald-green hiddenite, prismatic crystals, often of great size.
Lithium is an element valuable for the production of glass, aluminum products, and batteries. It is mined from ores of petalite LiAl(Si2O5)2, lepidolite K(Li,Al) Lithium is a silvery-white to grey alkali metal with a metalic lustre when fresh, but it is also highly reactive and in air quickly tarnishes to a dull silvery-grey and then black.
