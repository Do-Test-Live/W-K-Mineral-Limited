know about K Mineral Limited

We are a Hong Kong based company which has branches in Zambia (Gold Mines), Mozambique, Kenya, Brazil, Madagascar (KS Gems SARL). We are one of the world's foremost suppliers on exotic-colored rough gemstones. We have mining production, business operations and subsidiaries across four continents of the world supporting local people in the mine areas ... Our group was founded more than 25 years ago, and we focus on wholesales, distribution of rough colored gemstones as well as specimens. We are well known in the gemstone markets for supplying High Quality Rough Aquamarine, Morganite, Sphene, Tourmalines, Paraiba, Apatite neon, etc... We have gemstones in our ready stocks and we could also take orders and deliver them on time to manufacturers, jewelers, privates buyers and gemstones lovers, this granted us our reputation in the gemstone industries.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be one of the biggest suppliers of semi-precious stones in the gemstone market. The complete process of sourcing and marketing is all done by ourselves. Our strength lies on sourcing raw material (semi-precious stones) directly from the mines which allows us to provide excellent quality gemstones and services to our customers. K MINERAL is capable of supplying all kinds of semi-precious stones in regular basis from different mines…
