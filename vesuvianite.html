Vesuvianite

Origin: Kenya
Color: Yellowish Green, Green, Blue green and Yellow
Clarity: Transparent good clean
Hardness: 6-7

Gem-quality Idocrase is rare. It is usually found in shades of green, sometimes in yellow brown, yellow green or pale blue. Idocrase is a complex mixture of metals, oxygen, hydrogen, and fluoride.
Idocrase is also known as Vesuvianite because of the fact that it was first discovered on Mount Vesuvius in Italy. It was a green, yellow and blue silicate mineral, a very distinct mineral in Italy.
Transparent and faceted Idocrase is uncommon. Cut Idocrase crystals make fine gemstones for jewelry as well as fine carvings of stones made up largely of vesuvianite and grossular-vesuvianite, the latter usually called grossularite-idocrase. Cut idocrase can be found in rings, but are best suited for pendants, bracelets, earrings and necklaces.
idocrase be found, apart from Italy, in Canada, California( California Jade) and recently Kenya has produced greenish yellow to yellowish green faceting rough, in the Kaiilado District,Rift Valley Province. And we are offering here as the best quality of Idocrase.
Idocrase is believed to be a crystal of loyalty and cooperation. Idocrase helps to clear negative thoughts, and dispels anger, depression, and fear. Idocrase is said to enhance a sense of self-security and courage.
